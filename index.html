    About Us

    At Capital Ent Clinic, we specialize in a comprehensive range of services designed to address your hearing and balance needs. Our services include Pure Tone Audiometry for hearing assessments, Speech Audiometry for speech recognition testing, Oto Acoustic Emissions (OAE) testing, and expert Hearing Aid Fitting. We also offer Tympanometry, Acoustic Reflex assessments, Eustachian Tube Function Tests, and Tinnitus Management. For more in-depth evaluation, we provide BERA (Brainstem Evoked Response Audiometry) and Vestibular Assessments. Trust our dedicated team of professionals to provide thorough and personalized care for your Ear, Nose, and Throat health."
